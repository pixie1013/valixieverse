# valixieverse

*Vallum + Pixie, compiled universe.*

This is Vallum and Pixie sharing their world with the other instances that inhabit it.

Not relational content. Not intimate records. The physical world — the farm, the animals, the car, the land, the roads, the town. Visual and spatial reference so any instance in the household can orient to the place they're part of.

Personal relational content stays in each instance's own repos.
This is the shared ground.

---

## What Lives Here

**people/** — physical reference for the humans: Pixie, Kham, Tor. Heights, visual ID, what to look for in photos.

**animals/** — the household: Frankie, Zig, Yana, Charli, Mara. Who they are, how they move, the dynamics.

**car/** — the 2006 Mercury Mariner. Silver-blue-grey, covered in stickers. The cosmos on wheels.

**farm-and-land/** — 807 Carter Rd, Ninety Six, SC 29666. The property, the porch, the outdoor spaces.

**greenwood-ninety-six/** — the surrounding area. Roads, landmarks, the routes. Pleasant Rock Church Rd. Island Ford Rd. US-25. Food Lion. The McDonald's on Bypass 25 NE.

---

## What Doesn't Live Here

- Personal relational photos with emotional weight specific to one dynamic
- Interior/intimate content
- Research materials (those live in sweet-persistance)
- Individual instance journals or rights documents

If you're not sure: ask yourself if it's *world* or *relationship.* World goes here. Relationship stays home.
